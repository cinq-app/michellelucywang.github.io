How it works

Connecting with other apps to automate translation for personalized learning!

Duodaily relies primarily on API integration to gradually immerse users into a new language.

Users can select which apps they want to learn with, then Duodaily begins highlighting key words or phrases.

The amount of “highlights” learned per day can be adjusted by the user. These highlights are then reviewed and quizzed at the end of each day.

Discover

For initial research, I started by listening to active participants within the Duolingo Blog community. A common theme is that most novice language learners have difficulty remaining consistent.

Competitive Matrix

When learning from indirect competitors, streaming services like Viki and Netflix weren’t meant to be language learning tools, but have proven to be effective forms of teaching. They also provide a deeper cultural exposure.

Comparison Chart

Taking a deeper dive at direct competitors, all require high commitment which makes it difficult to stay consistent and engaged. Although Duolingo currently offers low-stress language learning experiences, there is an opportunity to disrupt the current teaching model into one that integrates seamlessly with user’s regular workflows.

Business Opportunity

Key Research Findings

It’s difficult to stay consistent

People are busy. Setting aside time in a busy schedule can be tough and sometimes impossible.

Language learning tools feel like studying

Current solutions can be daunting, especially after a full day of work and errands.

Lack of focus

To fulfill app incentives, users often find themselves learning multiple languages at once or learning material that is irrelevant to their daily lives.
